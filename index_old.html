Underwater Maritime Monitoring

A joint initiative by Dubai Future Foundation and Khalifa University to develop intelligent robotic systems for underwater and surface maritime monitoring.

Explore Project Meet the Team
Dubai Future Foundation
Khalifa University

Project Overview

Developing a heterogeneous robotic marine monitoring system that combines underwater and surface autonomous platforms.

Advanced Robotics

Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Unmanned Surface Vehicles (USVs) equipped with cutting-edge technology.

Maritime Applications

  • Offshore inspections
  • Environmental monitoring
  • Marine asset management

Advanced Capabilities

  • Mapping & localization
  • Navigation systems
  • Perception technologies
Duration April 1, 2025 - March 31, 2028
Location Dubai Future Labs & Khalifa University, UAE

Project Objectives

01

Heterogeneous Robotic System

Develop a marine monitoring system integrating USVs and ROVs for comprehensive coverage.

02

Integrated Perception

Design advanced perception systems using multi-sensor fusion for enhanced environmental understanding.

03

AI-Driven Vision

Apply AI-driven computer vision and Generative AI techniques for complex marine inspection tasks.

04

System Validation

Validate the system through high-fidelity simulations and real-world experiments in maritime environments.

Work Packages

Five comprehensive work packages covering all aspects of the project

WP1

Multi-Modal Underwater ROV & USV

Design, development, and integration of robotic platforms

  • Mechanical and electrical design
  • Communication and control architecture
  • Onboard computing and sensor integration
WP2

System Navigation

Development of robust navigation capabilities

  • Localization and guidance in maritime environments
  • Simulation-based algorithm testing
  • Field experiments for validation
WP3

System Perception

Advanced perception and understanding of underwater environments

  • Image and video denoising and enhancement
  • Object detection and tracking
  • Multi-sensor data fusion
WP4

Test and Validation

End-to-end validation of the system

  • Simulation campaigns
  • Controlled field trials in UAE waters
  • Performance benchmarking
WP5

Dissemination

Sharing outcomes and maximizing impact

  • Scientific and technical publications
  • Stakeholder demonstrations
  • Technology patenting
