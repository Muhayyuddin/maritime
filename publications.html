Research Focus Areas

Key research themes driving our publication strategy

Autonomous Robotics

Development of intelligent robotic systems for underwater and surface maritime operations.

  • Multi-platform coordination
  • Autonomous navigation
  • Adaptive control systems

Computer Vision & AI

Advanced perception and artificial intelligence for underwater environment understanding.

  • Underwater image enhancement
  • Object detection and tracking
  • Machine learning algorithms

Marine Systems Engineering

Design and optimization of marine robotic platforms and their operational systems.

  • Platform design optimization
  • Sensor integration
  • Environmental adaptation

Data Analytics & Monitoring

Advanced data processing and analytics for maritime environmental monitoring applications.

  • Real-time data processing
  • Environmental pattern analysis
  • Predictive monitoring systems
