Project Objectives

Four key research goals driving our underwater maritime monitoring system

Home / Objectives
01

Heterogeneous Robotic System

Develop a comprehensive marine monitoring system integrating Unmanned Surface Vehicles (USVs) and Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) for complete coverage of both surface and underwater environments.

Key Components:

  • Multi-platform integration architecture
  • Seamless communication protocols between USVs and ROVs
  • Coordinated mission planning and execution
  • Real-time data sharing and synchronization
02

Integrated Perception System

Design and implement advanced perception systems utilizing multi-sensor fusion techniques to enhance environmental understanding and situational awareness in challenging maritime conditions.

Technologies:

  • Advanced sonar and acoustic sensors
  • High-resolution underwater cameras
  • Environmental monitoring sensors
  • GPS and inertial navigation systems
03

AI-Driven Computer Vision

Apply state-of-the-art artificial intelligence, computer vision, and Generative AI techniques to enable autonomous decision-making and complex marine inspection capabilities.

AI Applications:

  • Real-time object detection and classification
  • Anomaly detection in marine structures
  • Predictive analytics for maintenance scheduling
  • Autonomous navigation and path planning
04

System Validation & Testing

Conduct comprehensive validation through high-fidelity simulations and extensive real-world experiments in relevant maritime environments to ensure system reliability and performance.

Validation Approach:

  • Physics-based simulation environments
  • Controlled testing in UAE coastal waters
  • Performance benchmarking against industry standards
  • Iterative system refinement and optimization
