Project Overview

Developing a heterogeneous robotic marine monitoring system that combines underwater and surface autonomous platforms.

Advanced Robotics

Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Unmanned Surface Vehicles (USVs) equipped with cutting-edge technology.

Maritime Applications

  • Offshore inspections
  • Environmental monitoring
  • Marine asset management

Advanced Capabilities

  • Mapping & localization
  • Navigation systems
  • Perception technologies
